ABOUT

What we stand for — cocktails, glocal plates & rooftop energy.

In the heart of South Bangalore, Bramble comes to life — inspired by the berry and built around a beautiful affair between crafted cocktails and glocal cuisine. Two floors: cozy indoor dining below and a spectacular rooftop view above.

A two-floor experience

The space is elevated with aesthetic furniture and neon eclectic artwork, creating a lively vibe that beckons to be discovered. Our menu blends global techniques with local favorites — from Kundapur Ghee Roast, Nethli Fry, Curd Rice Bonda — to Italian, Asian, Mediterranean and modern Indian creations.
